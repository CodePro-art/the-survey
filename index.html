Hello there, young student!

We, here at the "Priel Cohanim Corporation for Fun Courses" (also known as PCCFC),
would like to officially welcome you to "Interactivity in Fiction"—the latest addition to our ever-expanding collection of elective courses.

"Interactivity in Fiction" is a unique course that allows students to determine the course contents,
including the topics taught and the primary and secondary sources discussed in class.

To provide such a service for our students, we use a state-of-the-art, incredibly complicated,
and not-at-all-easy-to-comprehend system called "The Survey."

"The Survey" identifies students' interests and motivations by collecting their
answers to simple questions.

Then, our talented staff of engineers—which includes a psychologist, a monkey with a top hat,
and that weird Linguistics teacher you know—analyzes these answers to produce a course
experience individually tailored to each student and to the class as a whole.

We ask that you take 5 minutes of your time to fill in "The Survey."

Please answer the following questions sincerely, so our system can accurately predict
and assign activities best suited for you and your course experience.
